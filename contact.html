Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What documents do I need for vehicle purchase?

For vehicle purchase, you'll need your original CNIC, driver's license, and proof of income. For financing, additional documents including bank statements and employment verification may be required.

How does the rental process work?

Our rental process is simple: 1) Choose your vehicle and rental period, 2) Provide required documents (CNIC, driver's license), 3) Pay security deposit, 4) Enjoy your luxury vehicle. We offer delivery and pickup services.

What is your trade-in valuation process?

We conduct a comprehensive evaluation including: vehicle inspection, market analysis, service history review, and current demand assessment. You'll receive a competitive offer within 24 hours.

Do you offer test drives?

Yes, we encourage test drives for all our vehicles. You can schedule a test drive through our website, by phone, or by visiting our showroom. Our sales representatives will accompany you for the test drive.
